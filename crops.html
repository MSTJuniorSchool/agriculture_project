Spring onions

Diseases and pests

    Thrips

    Thrips is a deadly pest.
    Thrips feed under the leaf folds and in the protected inner leaves near the bulb.
    This enables thrips to cause a stunted growth on onions making them small in size.

    Treatment for thrips

    Thrips as a pest can be avoided by;
    By spraying using insecticidal soaps; acephate(orthene),bifenthrin,imidacloprid.

    Neck rot

    Neck rot is a disease that attacks the neck and bulbs of an onion.
    The bulbs are usually first affected at the neck.
    Although the decay can spread downwards to affect the whole bulb.
    It is a fungal disease.

    Treatment for neck rot

    By spraying using mancozeb.
    By spraying using chlorothalonil.

    Purple blot

    Purlpe blot affects the leaves of an onion.
    It leaves purple markings on the leaves of an onion.
    It makes leaves of onions wilt.

    Treatment for purple blot

    By spraying using chlorothalonil.
    By spraying using mancozeb.
