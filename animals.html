    Types of cattle feeds

    Forages

    Concentrates

    Succulents

    Forages

    Forages are cattle feeds with a high fibre content and low moisture content.
    Examples of forages are;
  • Pasture
  • Silage
  • Hay

    • Concentrates

    Concentrates are cattle feeds with a high value content.
    They are also called manufactured cattle feeds.
    Example of concentrates are;
  • Maize brand
  • Cotton seed cake
  • Blood meal

    • Succulents

    Succulents are cattle feeds with a high moisture content and a low fibre content.
    The examples are;
  • Dried beet pulp
  • Green oats
  • Peas
  • Pumpkin
